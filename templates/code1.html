It has always been a tough choice for students to choose the courses in different semesters where in there is the possibility to score good grades apart from the interest in the course for a future career.
Banasthali Vidyapith offers a variety of disciplinary and open elective courses with mandatory courses in the final semester.
Hence, choosing the courses based on the verbal recommendation from the seniors, instructors, and fellow mates becomes a hectic task. To ease this course recommendation process for the upcoming semester, we have developed a system that deploys simple yet powerful recommendation techniques. It is a system that takes your input and then, it outputs the top 2 courses that you can choose based on your interest.
